## Blog

### Steps to generate SEO Optimized Blog

1. Research about topic

- Gap questions
- Copetitors keywords
- Top keywords

2. Generate a blog based on outline

- Outline
- Main content
- Conclusion
- Refrences

3. Content Generation ( Section by Section) Example below:

- Intro
- concept section
- internals
- examples
- pitfals
- conclustion
- refrences

  4.Style (User voice and tone) Inforcement

- simple and easy to understand
- explain like you are teaching to beginners
- short paragraphs ( mostyl prefer points)
- conversation but precise

5. Proofread

- replace banned words list given by user
- repetition detector
- sentence length detector

6. Humanize

- make it more conversational and engaging
- inject sentences like ( this fails when, i have seen this will break, people assusme x but, )
- remove AI words/ phrases

7. Generate SEO friendly title,description and keywords

- title should be less than 60 characters long
- description should be less than 155 characters long
- keywords should be 10 keywords
- Internal linking for existing articles
